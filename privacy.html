Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 27, 2026

1. Overview

Clawup ("we", "our", or "us") is a companion mobile application for remotely controlling your local OpenClaw instance. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Clawup is independently developed and is not affiliated with the open-source OpenClaw project.

2. Information We Collect

Clawup does not require account registration. We collect minimal data:

  • Connection settings: Relay server URL, Room ID, and Auth Token you configure — stored locally on your device only.
  • App preferences: Theme and language settings — stored locally on your device only.
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash data collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage events (screen views, feature usage) via Firebase Analytics to improve the app.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality.
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs.
  • To understand how users interact with the app and improve the user experience.

4. Data Storage & Security

All connection credentials (Relay URL, Room ID, Auth Token) are stored exclusively on your local device using the operating system's secure storage. We never transmit these credentials to our servers.

Messages you send to your OpenClaw instance are transmitted via the relay server you configure. We do not control, store, or access the content of your messages.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each subject to their own privacy policy:

  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting (Firebase Privacy)
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics (Firebase Privacy)

6. Children's Privacy

Clawup is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
privacy@clawup.app

隐私政策
最后更新：2026 年 2 月 27 日

1. 概述

Clawup（以下简称"我们"）是一款用于远程控制您本地 OpenClaw 实例的配套移动应用。我们高度重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们收集哪些信息、如何使用，以及您享有的权利。

Clawup 为独立开发项目，与开源项目 OpenClaw 没有直接关联。

2. 我们收集的信息

Clawup 无需账号注册。我们仅收集极少量数据：

  • 连接设置：您配置的 Relay 服务器地址、房间 ID 和认证令牌——仅存储于您的本地设备。
  • 应用偏好：主题和语言设置——仅存储于您的本地设备。
  • 崩溃报告：通过 Firebase Crashlytics 收集的匿名崩溃数据，用于帮助我们修复问题。
  • 使用分析：通过 Firebase Analytics 收集的匿名使用事件（页面访问、功能使用情况），用于改善应用体验。

我们不会收集您的姓名、电子邮件、电话号码或地理位置等个人信息。

3. 信息的使用方式

  • 提供并维护应用的核心功能。
  • 诊断崩溃并修复问题。
  • 了解用户如何与应用交互，以改善使用体验。

4. 数据存储与安全

所有连接凭证（Relay 地址、房间 ID、认证令牌）均通过操作系统的安全存储机制，仅存储在您的本地设备上。我们不会将这些凭证传输至我们的服务器。

您发送给 OpenClaw 实例的消息，通过您自行配置的 Relay 服务器进行传输。我们不控制、存储或访问您消息的内容。

5. 第三方服务

我们使用以下第三方服务，各自适用其独立的隐私政策：

  • Firebase Crashlytics — 崩溃报告（Firebase 隐私政策）
  • Firebase Analytics — 匿名使用分析（Firebase 隐私政策）

6. 儿童隐私

Clawup 不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

7. 政策变更

我们可能会不定期更新本隐私政策。重大变更时，我们将更新页面顶部的日期。继续使用本应用即表示您接受更新后的政策。

8. 联系我们

如您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系我们：
privacy@clawup.app
